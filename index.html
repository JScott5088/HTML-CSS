Telling a story — with infographic data.

Introducing GoVo or Go Voter, Go! The first app rooted in political data using infographics to help voters connect the dots. Today, it's tough getting all the right facts, especially during election campaigns. So let us do it. We're pretty good at it. We promise!

A Unique UI

Feel smart, because you are! Our simple and beautiful UI easily displays candidate voting history, sponsors, investments, policies/laws created, conflicts of interest, community impact, and more.

Timely Updates

Timely and synchronized updates on local, state, and national campaigns and elections. We work with the best data to ensure that you're 100% informed. We're your personal political analyst.

Research Backed

We talked with voters and their main grievance is not knowning enough about candidates. From voting history to sponsors, you'll know how each candidate can really affect your quality of life.

Follow the Trail

Money makes the world go round. At GoVo, we're committed to showing the influence it has on U.S. elections and law makers. It affects our politicians and the laws they create.

Download it — Simple as 1, 2, 3

1

Download the app, sign up with a free, non-invasive account based on your interests.

2

Get matched to candidates based on your interests, and learn some facts to connect the dots.

3

Be informed with fact-based knowledge and feel confident to vote at the polls.
